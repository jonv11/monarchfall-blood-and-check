# Jira Conventions and Process Guide

## Purpose
Provide a standardized approach for creating, managing, and completing work items in MFBC (Monarchfall: Blood & Check). Following these conventions ensures consistency across the project and reduces friction for both human contributors and AI agents alike.

## Scope
Applies to all Jira work items in MFBC, including Epics, Features, Tasks, and Subtasks.

## When to Use
Use this index when creating, reviewing, or updating Jira work items in MFBC.
